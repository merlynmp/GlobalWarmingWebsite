Global Warming

The purpose of this website is to educate individuals about global warming. One of the main causes is greenhouse gases, about which you can learn more on this website. There are many detrimental effects that are affecting us right now and will in the future. However, there are various actions we can take to FIGHT IT!


What is Global Warming?

Global Warming is the increased temperature that the Earth is facing. Every decade for the last 40 years, the temperature has been rising .32 degrees Fahrenheit. As a result, the Earth is literally getting heated.


Why is it happening?

The Greenhouse Effect is a natural process that warms up the earth. Below is an explanation

  • The Sun's radiation travels toward the Earth.
  • Some of the energy is reflected and the rest is absorbed by the Earth's surface.
  • The earth emits the heat back into space.
  • However, most of the heat is trapped by greenhouse gases, warming the planet.

What are GreenHouse gases???

Greenhouse gases are gases that absorb infrared radiation.

Natural sources:

  • Volcano eruptions contribute to Carbon Dioxide in the atmosphere.
  • The decomposition of organic matter and digestion of plant matter creates Methane.

Anthropogenic sources:

  • Burning of fossil fuels produces carbon dioxide which enters the atmosphere.
  • Overirrigated fields create low-oxygen environments that produce methane and nitrous oxide.
  • Synthetic fertilizers and manures create nitrates in soil converted to nitrous oxide.
  • Raising livestock produces Methane.
  • Deforestation: When trees are destroyed through burning and decomposition, releasing carbon dioxide into the environment.
  • The decomposition of organic material in landfills produces Methane.
  • Methane gas leaks.
  • Industrial production of CFCs which are used to cool air conditioners, freezers, and refrigerators.
